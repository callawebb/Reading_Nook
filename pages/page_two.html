Get Started

Challenge Yourself

Set a goal Everyone has to start somewhere and somewhere is a fun place to start! By setting a goal for yourself you can help form habit that becomes a natural part of your life. A goal can be to read 30 minutes before bed, read a month, or try venturing into a new genre. The point of this is fun and cultivating a soothing place for your mind, so try to make a goal that is fun and approachable for you.

Another Note.....

Creating a reading spot is helpful to start your reading process. A spot that you feel comfortable and can focus on retaining new information. I'm always interested where people find the their reading spots, hence the name of the website, and find inspiration from others. Coffeeshops, in nature, on your couch, or in your backyard. The list is endless. I wish you luck!
