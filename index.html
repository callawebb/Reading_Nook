How my love for reading sprouted! As far my my memory reaches, I have clear memories of my parents reading to me before bed. This rountine grew to independent reading when I wanted to relax, kill time while traveling, and explore infinite amounts of topics. Reading is one of my greatest joys and I have stacks of books "to-read" and read around my house. I love it and love what it brings to my life.

Why you should start reading for fun!

Reading has many benefits, it teaches us new vocabulary, improves our focus and imagination, makes us smarter and most importantly improves memory. Those who are habitual in reading feel comforted with books. It soothes and relieves tension and loneliness. People often get tired in their daily routines.

Other Thoughts....

In our technological age, creating imagery in our own mind is a powerful tool to keep our inner worlds alive and centered. Giving ourselves and our neurological systems reprieve from a busy world is an unexpected form of self love.
